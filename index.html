We are Dollars For Scholars

We are Dollars for Scholars. We are a non-profit 501 (C)(3) organization company that aims to improve the lives of college students across the world that are unable to live up to their full potential in college because they have to worry about paying for their college expenses.

Why Work With Us

We are hoping to improve the college experience so that students can get the full experience of it instead of constantly worry about college expenses.

This donation service is also beneficial to the donors as they receieve a tax deductible for when they file their taxes.
